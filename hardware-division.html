🛠️ Andes Space Hardware Division

Architecting the physical framework and electronics for outer space

Mission & Objectives

Our hardware division designs, simulates, and manufactures the physical systems of our CubeSats. Dealing with extreme temperature swings and high radiation requires highly rigorous hardware design and rigorous component picking.

Engineering Core Subsystems

1. Electrical Power Subsystems (EPS)

We work on high-efficiency solar panel layouts, battery management networks, and deployment modules to guarantee clean power distribution across all operational phases.

2. Attitude Determination and Control Systems (ADCS)

Integrating magnetorquers, reaction wheels, and sun sensors to steer the nanosatellite accurately towards targeted areas on earth.

3. Structures & Testing

Machining standard 1U/3U structures out of space-grade Aluminum 7075. Testing designs for extreme vibrations, thermal vacuums, and physical structural strain profiles.

Hardware Capabilities & Tools

  • Design & Simulation: Altium Designer, KiCAD, SolidWorks ANSYS
  • Manufacturing: Multi-layer PCB manufacturing, CNC milling, SMD reflow soldering
  • Space Standards: Adhering to ESA ECSS and NASA CubeSat Design Specifications
