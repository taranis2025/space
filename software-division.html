💻 Andes Space Software Division

Developing the brains and intelligence of our orbital nanosatellites

Mission & Objectives

Our mission is to build highly reliable, fault-tolerant software infrastructures capable of operating autonomously in harsh space environments. From low-level microcontrollers to advanced ground station servers, code keeps our equipment alive.

Core Technical Segments

1. On-Board Computer (OBC) Firmware

We implement specialized Real-Time Operating Systems (RTOS) to manage scheduling, thread priorities, and peripheral controls ensuring telemetry calculations are downlinked with microsecond accuracy.

2. Telemetry & Communications Protocols

Designing optimal data packets using custom encoding/decoding frameworks to maximize data transmission speeds across VHF/UHF and S-band frequencies.

3. Simulation & Machine Learning

Simulating digital twin dynamics of the CubeSat to predict orbital paths, magnetic alignments, and kinetic reactions under atmospheric drag conditions.

Our Technology Stack

  • Languages: C/C++, Rust, Python, MATLAB
  • Environments & RTOS: FreeRTOS, Linux Embedded, ROS2
  • Data Formats: CCSDS Protocols, Protocol Buffers
